My 2026 Gear Loadout

Every year, I re-evaluate what goes into my camera bag. As I've shifted more towards high-end commercial and wedding photography, my philosophy has changed from "carry everything" to "carry the absolute best tools for the specific job."

My main bodies are currently dual full-frame mirrorless cameras. The shift to mirrorless has been massive for silent shooting during quiet ceremony moments and reliable eye-autofocus.

As for glass, I rarely use zoom lenses anymore. My kit revolves around three prime lenses: a 35mm for environmental storytelling, a 50mm for standard portraits, and an 85mm for those beautiful, compressed, creamy backgrounds. Less weight, more creativity.
